# 细胞器分格大图 prompt — 5 sheets × 6 panels

## 共用 Style Anchor（所有 5 条 prompt 头部插入）

> **Style Anchor v2** — Vibrant semi-realistic scientific illustration, painterly textures, **BOLD VOLUMETRIC 3D-sculpted forms** (every element reads as a solid sculpted mass with strong chiaroscuro shading, NOT flat drawing, NOT thin wisp, NOT delicate line art). Rich polychromatic palette — **at least 4 distinct saturated hues per subject**. Interior parts full, plump, each a distinct 3D volume. Textbook-illustrative but colorful. Soft even directionless studio lighting with visible volumetric shading. Plain warm off-white paper-textured background.
>
> **Layout constraint (this sheet)** — 3×2 grid of 6 equal square panels on one canvas. Each panel shows ONE isolated organelle centered at ~70% panel fill, consistent cream paper background across the entire canvas (no borders, no frames, no gridlines, no captions). Organelles do not overlap panels. No text anywhere.
>
> Aspect ratio 3:2 (1536×1024).
